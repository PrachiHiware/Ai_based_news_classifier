AI-Powered News Article Classifier with History

  1. Classify a News Article

    1. Enter Article URL: On the homepage, You can input the URL of the news article you want to categorise in the box on the homepage.
    2. Submit the URL: Enter the URL and select "Classify" from the menu. The software will download the article's content, use AI to determine its category, and display the predicted category on the screen.
    3. Repeat Classification: By entering the URLs and selecting "Classify" each time, you can classify several articles at once.

  2. View Classification History

    1. Navigate to History:Click on the "History" item in the navigation menu to see a history of previous classification requests.
    2. History Page:Your browser will then direct you to a website with a table showing the history of your classification requests. The article URL, projected category, and request timestamp are all listed in columns in the table.
    3. Review History: Using the application, you can examine the whole history of categorization requests you've sent.
    4. Back to Classification: You can click the "Back to Classification" link at the bottom of the history page to return to the article categorization page.
